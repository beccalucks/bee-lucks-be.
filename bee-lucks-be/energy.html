Energy Consumption in the UK

By Becca Thomas Loux

Linked above is a visualisation of data showing UK energy usage from renewable resources.

It was originally submitted as an assessment project for my Data Viz module for my MSc Computational & Data Journalism at Cardiff University taught by Martin Chorley. The project uses Java Script and three libraries: DataWrapper, D3.js and Plot.ly. The individual charts are interactive and annotated, and display energy usage by source and occupation from the years 1990-2015.

As the third visualisation runs on a Python server, make sure you run one from the command line to see the small percentage of the UK's total energy consumption that comes from renewable resources.

Data for this project is from the Office for National Statistics (ONS).
